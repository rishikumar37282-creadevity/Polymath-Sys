The Biography

Dario Amodei is a physicist by training and an AI architect by choice. Educated at Princeton and Stanford, Amodei's academic background in statistical mechanics had a profound influence on his approach to neural networks.

Before his time at OpenAI, Amodei worked at Baidu and Google Brain. He led the research team that developed GPT-2 and GPT-3, but his primary concern remained the ethical alignment of these systems.

In 2021, along with his sister Daniela and several key researchers, he left OpenAI to found Anthropic, a "Public Benefit Corporation" dedicated to building safe, interpretable, and steerable AI such as Claude.

The Amodei Mental Model
Empirical Predictability
Predict the Future
Think of AI not as magic, but as a predictable physical system. If we understand the scaling laws today, we can predict the capabilities of models three years from now.
The Safety-Capability Loop
Never increase the capability of a model without simultaneously increasing our ability to explain and control it. Safety should be the catalyst for capability, not its enemy.
