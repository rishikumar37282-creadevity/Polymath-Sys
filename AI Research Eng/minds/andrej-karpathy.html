The Biography

Andrej Karpathy is as much a teacher as he is a world-class engineer. Born in Slovakia and educated at the University of Toronto and Stanford, Karpathy's PhD thesis under Fei-Fei Li was the first to bridge the gap between image analysis and natural language descriptions.

As the former Director of AI at Tesla, he led the move away from radar and LIDAR toward a pure "Tesla Vision" approach, proving that neural networks alone could navigate complex real-world environments at scale.

Karpathy is now dedicated to AI Education through his startup, Eureka Labs, where he creates some of the most refined and intuitive deep learning resources in existence, such as the "Zero-to-Hero" series.

Learning Paths & Strategies
The Karpathy Mental Model
Intuition Building
Build from Scratch
Never use a high-level library (like PyTorch or TensorFlow) until you have written the backpropagation logic for that same model by hand in pure NumPy.
Teaching is Mastering
If you can't explain a Transformer to a non-technical person using only basic metaphors, you don't understand it deeply enough.
Advice for Researchers
The Build Loop
Hyper-Focus on Gradients
AI isn't magic; it's just gradients. Study the gradient flow in your network. If the gradients aren't moving, the machine isn't "thinking."
Document the Process
Karpathy was one of the first to live-tweet and blog about his exact research steps. His advice: "The public log of your work is your greatest asset."
