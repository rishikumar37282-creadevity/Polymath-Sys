# Spine + Strategy
Phase 2 · Months 7–24
All four spikes you chose are real and compatible — but only if they're sequenced, not run in parallel. The spine is AI Product Builder.

## The Sequence
1. PyTorch + Deep DL
2. LLMs + RAG + Agents
3. MLOps + Systems
4. AI × Finance
5. Research Track

# PyTorch + DL
Months 7–9 (primary) + ongoing
PyTorch is the framework of choice for research and production in 2025.

## Karpathy — Neural Networks: Zero to Hero
6 videos building everything from scratch: micrograd, makemore, nanoGPT. By video 6 you will have built GPT-2 from scratch in pure Python.

## fast.ai Part 2 — Deep Learning Foundations to Stable Diffusion
Build Stable Diffusion from scratch. Teaches you how to read papers and implement them.

# LLMs + RAG + Agents
Months 9–12 · Primary spine
This is where your AI Product Builder spike monetizes.

2-hour video building GPT-2. Tokenization, embeddings, attention, the full training loop.

2024 book. Builds a GPT-like LLM from scratch in PyTorch. Covers fine-tuning with RLHF.
